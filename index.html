I am an NSERC postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of British Columbia working in the fields of cosmology and astrophysics. I study the origin and evolution of dark and visible matter in the universe through cosmological observations.

My research involves preparation and analysis of large data from astrophysical surveys. I perform a wide range of statistical analysis and visualization of the acquired data to extract cosmological information that I then use to study the predictions of theoretical astrophysical and cosmological models.
